\section*{Introduction}


The paper will be about
\begin{my_itemize}
\item how to register compounds in RDBMS
\item strategies for prescreening
\item fingerprinting and solid criteria about how to design fingerprints and what frequency distribution is best
\item Alternatives to Fingerprint prescreening
\item fastest implementations of substructure queries
\item and how to project that onto SQL databases
\item Stereochemistry searches
\item How to implement wild-card searching
\item Notoriously difficult cases, structures where many cartridges on the market fail
\end{my_itemize}
